Your friendly neighborhood sports bar and grill

Mesa Sports Grill (formerly Mesa Grill Company) is the latest restaurant concept created by Bellagio Pizzeria Mesa. Conveniently located in the east valley, Mesa Sports Grill was created to address the need for high-quality drinks, food, and fun at an affordable price in a pleasant atmosphere. Mesa Sports Grill is a family-owned, community-driven dining concept that appreciates the opportunity to embrace the inspiring flavors and strong appetites of this growing city and surrounding areas.
